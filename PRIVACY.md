# Privacy

LinguaMesh localization packs are data-only resources. They contain message identifiers,
translations, locale metadata, and generated platform resources; they contain no credentials,
provider content, telemetry, or user documents.

Generated files must preserve placeholder, plural, and escaping rules. Machine-generated locale
text remains marked as draft or unreviewed until a qualified language review is recorded. Report
privacy or unsafe-content defects through `SECURITY.md` without submitting private translation
content.
